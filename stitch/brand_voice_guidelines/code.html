Principles

  • Use active, evocative verbs

    Prefer "Illuminate the path" over "Showing you the way."

  • Maintain emotional distance

    Observe and describe rather than emoting or judging.

  • Prioritize rhythm over brevity

    A sentence should flow like a digital tide, not a staccato pulse.

cancel

Avoidances

  • No corporate buzzwords

    Avoid "Synergy," "Pivoting," or "Bandwidth."

  • Zero exclamation points

    Confidence is quiet. Exclamation denotes desperation.

  • Don't over-explain

    Assume the reader is as intelligent as the engine.
